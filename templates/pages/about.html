This website, rather than being only a movie introduction, is a project that has been emerged by a group of people consisting of various groups of jobs and making a new start to their life again as a refugee.

This training activity was led off by software expert Fatih DEMIR, came to here as a refugee, too in 2018, and by his idea to help people around like him. Mr. DEMIR, who intended to convey his knowledge to people, started a volunteer training activity. Firstly, he gave Python, Kivi, Django trainings to a group at 20 persons during 6 months. Those were the first seeds that provided a team, >>>Py[Coders] to be formed. Then, a new course was started with the ones who wanted to be volunteer trainers among the trained 20 trainees and a new group of 44 trainees.

Here is the we are the ones included in this second group. Hence, this website is our groduation project. This project that we did as a team of 10 persons, has been prepared by using django library. Further more, we had no software experiance and education before we came here. This web site is a concrate example illustrating our motivation and resoluteness to continue our career at software sector hereafter.

We thank ever so much to primary Mr.DEMIR, volunteers trainers, Stichting IPN , Metis Montessori Lyceum and all who had great effort in this training activity which is a new hope and a fresh start for us.
